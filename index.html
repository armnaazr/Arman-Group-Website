A bit about our history...

El Classico was born out of a deep passion for Mexican cuisine by someone who found it incredibly difficult to experience authentic Mexican food in Kuching, Malaysia. Despite the growing popularity of international flavors, the distinct tastes of Mexico were hard to come by in the region. This challenge sparked the idea to create a space where people could enjoy traditional Mexican dishes, made with fresh ingredients and rich cultural heritage. Driven by the desire to bring these bold, vibrant flavors to Kuching, we opened our doors in 2020. From tacos to tamales, enchiladas to fresh salsas, we’ve worked tirelessly to perfect each dish and share the warmth of Mexican cooking with our community. Our journey has been about more than just food; it’s about introducing a culture, a history, and a story that we’re proud to tell through every bite.
